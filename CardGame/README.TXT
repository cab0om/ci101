HOW TO START THIS PROJECT: Complete CardGame class

USER INSTRUCTIONS:
1.   Ask the player for their name
2.   The player is dealt three random cards
3.   For three rounds, they must exchange there cards in order to obtain the maximum points.
4.   If he/she has two cards from the same suit they get 5 extra points.
5.   If he/she has three cards from the same suit they get 10 extra points.
6.   The player can play as many games as he/she wants.
